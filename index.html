Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about homeopathy and our treatments

How does homeopathy work?

Homeopathy works on the principle of "like cures like," using highly diluted substances that would cause symptoms in a healthy person to treat similar symptoms in a sick person. It stimulates the body's natural healing processes to restore balance and health rather than merely suppressing symptoms.

The homeopathic approach considers each person's unique symptom pattern, constitution, and emotional state when prescribing remedies, making it a truly personalized form of medicine.

Is homeopathy safe for children and pregnant women?

Yes, homeopathic remedies are generally safe for children, pregnant women, and elderly people when prescribed by a qualified homeopath. The remedies are highly diluted and work gently without the side effects associated with conventional medications.

In fact, homeopathy is often particularly effective for children, whose vitality and response to natural treatment tends to be strong.

How long does homeopathic treatment take to show results?

The timeline for seeing results varies depending on the condition, its severity, and how long you've had it. Acute conditions may respond within hours or days, while chronic conditions might take weeks or months to show significant improvement.

Homeopathy addresses the root cause rather than just managing symptoms, which can take time but leads to more lasting results. During your treatment, Dr. Aditya will monitor your progress and adjust remedies as needed.

Can I take homeopathic remedies alongside conventional medicine?

In most cases, yes. Homeopathic remedies generally don't interact with conventional medications. However, it's important to inform both your homeopath and conventional doctor about all treatments you're receiving.

Never discontinue prescribed conventional medication without consulting the doctor who prescribed it. Dr. Aditya believes in an integrative approach and will work with your current treatment plan.

What should I expect during my first consultation?

The first consultation is comprehensive and typically lasts 45-60 minutes. Dr. Aditya will take a detailed case history, asking questions about your physical symptoms, medical history, mental and emotional state, lifestyle, and preferences.

This holistic approach helps in finding the most suitable remedy for your unique constitution and condition. You're encouraged to bring any relevant medical reports and a list of current medications to your first visit.
